Company Overview

Earth Search Consultant Limited (ESCL) is a strong and well established geological consulting and contracting firm that is dedicated to providing our customers with the utmost in service excellence, productivity and fair pricing. It has been our experience that building a successful reputation in the resource industry not only involves hard work and dedication, but a commitment to developing a strong client relationship. ESCL was registered in 2021 and has been actively involved in the industry since then. Our Experience and work in the geological industry tracks way back as early as 1995 when our founder of the company joined the geological industry. We pride ourselves on our extensive knowledge and experience of the Zambian geology and the mining regulatory framework.
